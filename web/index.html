Retention & frequency — are users building order frequency?

How this is measured Order-depth & repeat are measured on fixed windows from signup (D7 / D14 / D28), so every cohort is compared over the same elapsed time — not "as of today". A cohort is shown only once it is mature for the window (e.g. D28 needs the whole cohort to be ≥28 days old). That's why we anchor on the latest fully-matured cohort, , rather than last week's signups (whose 8th order simply can't exist yet). WoW here compares this cohort to the one before it.
Repeat is cumulative within the window — D7 ⊆ D14 ⊆ D28. A user whose 2nd order lands on day 10 counts in D14 & D28 but not D7, so the rate rises with the window.

Red / Yellow / Green logic (WoW; thresholds = assumptions)

Green — Keep
Orders & GMV ≥ −2% WoW AND DI/order not rising (≤ 0%) AND same-day activation Δ ≥ −1pp.
Red — Investigate / Revert
Orders or GMV ≤ −8% WoW; OR DI/order up > +5% while orders flat-to-down; OR same-day activation Δ ≤ −3pp and orders also down ≥2%. (Volume-aware — won't flag a city red while orders & GMV clearly grow.)
Yellow — Monitor / Optimize
Everything in between.
Low-signal
< 40 orders in the week — % swings unreliable, judged manually.
